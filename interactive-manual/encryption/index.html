# Encryption

How do you send someone a secret message?

Perhaps you create a secret code that only you and the intended recipient will understand.

The study and use of secret codes and ciphers is called "cryptography", and the process of applying these methods is called "encrypting".

One of the earliest examples we have from history is a simple substitution of one letter for another.

# Securing Data

In the modern digital landscape is it has become increasingly important to ensure that ALL data transferred is secured. Encryption can be found everywhere from checking your online bank account to sending an email. But what is it?

Encryption is a technique used to turn your data into unreadable and unusable gibberish in order to protect it from outsiders. Encryption method use mathematical algorithms and the stronger the encryption is, the more complex the mathematics is to solve, making your data increasingly safer from intruders. The only way to decode the encryption is with a key. There are many encryption algorithms out there and new ones are created often.
